About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the RoLi lab at the Max Planck Insititute for Biological Cybernetics.
I recently completed my PhD in the Laboratory of Brain Hearing and Behavior under the supervision of Dr. Stephen David where my studies were supported through a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship.

My primary research interests are in neural coding and development of software tools to support research and data management. I am particularly interested in how internal behavioral state modulates neural processing of sensory stimuli.
